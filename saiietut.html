GETTING STARTED

Common Questions

Find quick answers about our organization and community access.

What is SAIIE?
The South African Institute for Industrial Engineering (SAIIE) is the official professional body representing IE in South Africa. As the mother body, SAIIE focuses on networking, professional certification, and setting industry standards.
What is SAIIETUT?
We are the SAIIE Tshwane University of Technology student structure. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry reality. We’re here to support you with resources, networking events, and automated tools like this hub.
How can I join the community?
Joining is frictionless. You can click the 'Contact Us' button in the menu and reach out to register. Registration gives you dynamic access to the Academic Vault, our online suggestion box, and wellness events.
How can I join site visits?
Site visits are high-impact and managed professionally. You cannot join them by just emailing. You must go to the Library / Platform Hub section and book your spot using the dedicated form. You’ll receive a dynamic QR pass that is scanned on-site for automated attendance tracking.
