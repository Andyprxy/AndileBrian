Industrial Engineering is fundamentally about the optimization of complex processes. However, in a fast-paced production environment, optimization is impossible without transparency.

I recognized that traditional "end-of-month" reporting was too slow for proactive decision-making. By mastering BI tools like PowerBI, I bridge the gap between technical operations and executive strategy, turning static data into live, actionable intelligence.

Case Study: Farm Mania

Poultry Supply Chain Optimization

Farm Mania, a local poultry enterprise, faced significant challenges in tracking fluctuating inventory levels and identifying high-performing sales channels. Data was fragmented across various physical and digital touchpoints.

I engineered a live data ecosystem that synchronizes sales and inventory. This ensures that every chicken sold is accounted for in real-time, allowing the business to manage procurement cycles with 100% accuracy and zero waste.
